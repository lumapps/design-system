### Spacing and Alignment

Generally, you may use 1/3 of the width/height of the contained mark to determine the appropriate amount of space around the container. Information can be aligned to any edge of the container.

!['alignement example image'](demo/react/doc/brand/example-image.png)

Information may be middle aligned with the icon when the copy does not exceed the height of the container. When the information is longer than the height of the container, it may be top-aligned or stacked below and center-aligned.
