# Thumbnail <ReactStabilityFlag status="unstable" />

**Thumbnails display formatted images.**

## Sizes

Thumbnails come in 7 sizes (width): `xxs`, `xs`, `s`, `m`, `l`, `xl`, and unset.
Check [size design tokens](/product/design-tokens/size) for more details.

## Ratios

Thumbnails come in 4 ratios: `square`, `original`, `vertical`, and `horizontal`.

## Variants

Thumbnails come in two variants: `squared` and `rounded`
